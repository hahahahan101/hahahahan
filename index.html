KIP Kuliah volunteers are people who are committed and work voluntarily to make the KIP Kuliah program initiated by the government a success. The task of KIP Kuliah volunteers is to carry out outreach and assist high school students who have difficulty paying for college. The students targeted for socialization by the volunteers are high school students graduating in 2019, 2021, and 2022.

Volunteers are tasked with providing comprehensive information about KIP Kuliah, starting from the meaning, requirements, and how to get it to the benefits of having KIP Kuliah. Not only that, volunteers are also required to provide motivation so that students want to continue studying.

Even though they work voluntarily, of course there are several benefits that KIP Kuliah volunteers can get. Volunteers can have a wider network of connections, especially with fellow volunteers who come from various regions of Indonesia. Apart from that, volunteers will also get an experience e-certificate.

However, the most important thing to understand is that KIP Kuliah volunteers have indirectly helped produce a competent young generation who can advance the nation. So, volunteers have a big role in the world of education and can be agents of change in society.
